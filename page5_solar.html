Key Vocabulary:

  • Solar Energy - energy from the sun
  • Photosynthesis - the process by which plants use the sun to make food
  • Solar Panels - devices which capture and change solar energy into electricity

Solar energy is an important form of energy discussed on this website. Solar energy is energy from the sun. Almost all energy on Earth originally came from the sun. The sun provides both light and thermal (heat) energy. Plants use solar energy during photosynthesis to make a type of sugar they use for food. People are learning how to use solar energy, as well. An example of this would be solar panels.

Solar energy comes from the sun and can be captured with solar panels. Solar energy is absorbed by solar panels. The panels change solar energy into electricity. The electricity is used to power lights and provide heat for buildings. Like wind energy, solar energy is not always constant. Sometimes clouds block out the sunlight. Plus, solar energy is not available at night.
